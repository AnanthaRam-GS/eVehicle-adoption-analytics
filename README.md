# eVehicle-adoption-analytics

## Business Analytics of Electric Vehicle Markets

This repository contains the capstone project for the analysis of Electric Vehicle Adoption, focusing on the analysis of **global electric vehicle (EV) adoption, market performance, resale behavior, charging infrastructure readiness, and financial market signals**.  
The project combines secondary datasets from reliable public sources with primary survey data to derive actionable business insights.

---

## Project Objectives:

The primary objectives of this project are:
- To analyze demographic, economic, and infrastructural factors influencing EV adoption.
- To evaluate EV model-level sales performance and resale behavior.
- To study the relationship between real-world EV adoption metrics and the stock market performance of EV manufacturers.

---

## Datasets Used:

The project uses the following publicly available datasets:

- **Opendatabay ‚Äì Consumer EV Adoption Dataset**  
  Consumer preferences, awareness, and demographic attributes.

  EV registrations by model, year, and geographic region.

- **Opendatabay ‚Äì EV Market Dataset**  
  Aggregated EV sales growth and market expansion indicators.

- **IEA Global EV Data Explorer**  
  Global EV adoption trends and country-level statistics.

- **Global EV Sales Dataset (2010‚Äì2024)**  
  Long-term EV sales trends for time-series analysis.

- **Mendeley EV Adoption Survey Dataset**  
  Consumer attitudes, perceived barriers, and willingness to adopt EVs.

- **Open Charge Map Dataset**  
  Charging infrastructure location and density information.

was conducted using Google Forms to collect primary data on:
- EV awareness and adoption intent

- Infrastructure perception
- Market and investment outlook

---

## Methodology:

The project follows a structured **Business Analytics workflow**:
1. Data collection and preprocessing
2. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
3. Consumer and regional segmentation
4. Predictive and inferential modeling
5. Time-series and financial market analysis
6. Business interpretation and validation

The analysis is performed using **Python**, with libraries such as Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, and Scikit-learn.
